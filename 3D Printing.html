3D Printing Services

Our 3D printing services are simple, and easy to understand. You tell us what you want, we build it.
IBH has 2 3D printers that run every day, and have more coming in the future.
If what you want to print falls within our requirements (and most everything does) then just fill out a request, and we'll print it when we see it!
Pricing is simple. You pay for the filament you use. NOT the volume you use.

Requirements for our printers

  1. Dimmensions less than 10" cube
  2. Obeys the "45º Rule"
  3. 1 Color Print

Pricing

Prices below are prices before shipping. Shipping varies based on your location, and the size of your print
