IBH Tech - Black and White

IBH Tech is run by 2 students looking to make an impact in the world around them BEFORE they get their degrees.
Those Student's Bios, other work, and naratives are below.

The Story behind IBH

IBH  was started by Moses Chan and John Moosemiller, 2 friends with a passion to share the things that they love.
John and Moses have been friends since Jr. High in suburban Chicago, and continued to be friends through Highschool
After their Senior year in Highschool, the two were part of a group of friends that created Project LEAD an Illinois Non-Profit
IBH stemmed from the duo's interest in sharing their interests to a broad audience.

The goals of IBH

IBH wants to bring simplicity and customizability to small-scale tech-related products.
IBH isn't looking to make huge profits, or satisfy hungry capital sources. We do it for the love.
IBH will get you exactly what you want, how you want it, and when you want it, and will always look to satisfy the customer.
We're here for you. We'll get back to you. No funny business or sneaky practices.
Forever Black and White
